Featured Weed Species

🌿
High Threat

Palmer Amaranth

Aggressive broadleaf weed that competes heavily with crops for nutrients and water. Resistant to multiple herbicides.

🌱
High Threat

Giant Ragweed

Large broadleaf weed that can grow over 15 feet tall, significantly reducing crop yields through competition.

🌾
Medium Threat

Johnson Grass

Perennial grass weed with extensive rhizome system. Difficult to control once established.

🍀
Medium Threat

Wild Clover

Nitrogen-fixing legume that can compete with crops but also provides some soil benefits.

🌻
Low Threat

Common Dandelion

Perennial broadleaf weed with deep taproot. Generally manageable with proper timing.

🌿
Low Threat

Chickweed

Small annual broadleaf weed that thrives in cool, moist conditions. Easy to control.
